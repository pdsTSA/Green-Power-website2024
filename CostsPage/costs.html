Costs of Green Energy

There are many different types of green power ranging from wind power to solar power. There are pros and cons to each renewable energy source, and one of the pros and cons to each type of renewable energy source is the cost. Each type of renewable energy source has a different cost, different tax rebates, different amount of tax credits and different regulations. Tax rebates are getting tax money back and tax credits can reduce the amount of taxs that people have to pay.
